Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can you respond to a security incident?

Our SOC operates 24/7 with incident response times of under 15 minutes for critical threats. Emergency response teams can be deployed within 2-4 hours depending on location.

What compliance frameworks do you support?

We provide compliance solutions for ISO 27001, SOC 2, NIST CSF, PCI DSS, HIPAA, GDPR, CIS Controls, and FedRAMP. Our experts help with gap assessments, implementation, and ongoing compliance management.

Do you offer penetration testing services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive penetration testing including network, web application, mobile app, cloud infrastructure, and social engineering assessments. Reports include executive summaries and detailed remediation guidance.

Can you help with cloud security?

Absolutely. Our cloud security services cover AWS, Azure, and GCP environments. We provide security architecture reviews, configuration assessments, CSPM implementation, and ongoing monitoring.

What makes Matrica Networks different?

Our combination of proprietary tools (Shell IDE, Psychy OS, Sygnil), expert team, and focus on advanced threats sets us apart. We provide custom solutions tailored to each client's unique security landscape.

Do you provide training services?

Yes, we offer cybersecurity awareness training, technical training for IT teams, and specialized courses on threat hunting, incident response, and security architecture.
